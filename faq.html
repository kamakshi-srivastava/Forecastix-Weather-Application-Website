Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


1. What is the source of the weather data?

We use the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch real-time and forecasted weather data.

2. How often is the weather data updated?

Weather data is typically updated every 10 minutes depending on the city and data type.

3. Is the app free to use?

Yes, our weather app is completely free for personal use.

4. Can I check the weather for any city in the world?

Yes! Just enter the city name, and the app will show the current weather conditions.

5. How accurate is the weather information?

OpenWeatherMap provides reliable weather data, though minor variations may occur.

6. What kind of weather details can I see?

You can view temperature, humidity, wind speed, air pressure, and more.

7. Can I view the weather forecast for the week?

Yes, our app includes a multi-day forecast feature on the "Weather Forecast" page.

8. How do I change the temperature unit from Celsius to Fahrenheit?

You can switch units from the settings or the user profile section if available.

9. Why is the weather for my location not showing correctly?

This may happen due to incorrect location input or temporary API data delays.

10. Can I use the app in multiple languages?

Currently, the app supports English. Multilingual support may be added in the future.

11. Can I access this weather app offline?

No, an active internet connection is required to fetch the latest weather data.

12. How do I allow the app to use my current location?

You can enable location permissions through your browser or device settings.

13. What happens if I enter an invalid city name?

The app will show an error message and prompt you to enter a valid city.

14. Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes, all features are accessible without needing to sign up or log in.

15. Is my data safe when I use this app?

Absolutely. We prioritize user privacy and do not share your information with third parties.

16. How do I report a bug or issue with the app?

You can reach out via the "Contact Us" page to report any issues or suggestions.

17. Can I access historical weather data?

Currently, we display current and forecast data only. Historical data may be added in the future.

18. Is the app available as a mobile app too?

Not yet, but we are working on a mobile version of the app.

19. What should I do if the app is not loading properly?

Try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. If issues persist, contact support.
