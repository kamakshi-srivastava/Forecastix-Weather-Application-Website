Usage Guide for Forecastix – Your Personal Weather Assistant!


Welcome to the Forecastix usage guide! This page will help you understand how to navigate through the app and make the most out of its features.



1. How to Check the Weather


  1. Step 1: Open the Forecastix app on your device.
  2. Step 2: On the homepage, you’ll find a search bar at the top of the screen.
  3. Step 3: Enter the name of the city or location where you want to check the weather. You can enter a city name, state, or country.
  4. Step 4: Press the search icon, and the app will display the current weather details for that location, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.


2. Understanding the Weather Data


Once the weather data is displayed, you’ll see various sections:

  • Current Temperature: Shows the current temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit, depending on your settings.
  • Humidity: Displays the percentage of humidity in the air.
  • Wind Speed: Shows the speed of the wind (in km/h or mph).
  • Air Quality Index: The level of air pollution in the area (Good, Moderate, Unhealthy, etc.).
  • Hourly Forecast: You can also view hourly weather predictions for the next 12 or 24 hours.


3. Changing the Unit of Measurement


You can switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit or choose to display the wind speed in km/h or mph:

  • Go to the Settings page by clicking on the gear icon at the top right.
  • In the Units section, select your preferred units for temperature and wind speed.


4. Setting Your Location


If you want to quickly check the weather for your current location, simply:

  • Tap the Location button (usually a pin or GPS icon) on the homepage.
  • The app will access your device’s GPS to show the current weather conditions for your location.


5. Checking the Forecast for Multiple Cities


You can search and view weather data for multiple cities:

  • On the homepage, you’ll find a list or a button to Add Cities.
  • Search for different cities and save them to view their weather quickly whenever needed.


6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


How accurate is the weather data?
Our data comes from trusted weather providers like OpenWeather, ensuring you get accurate and real-time weather updates.

Can I use this app offline?
The app requires an active internet connection to fetch live weather data. However, you can still view previously fetched data if you have a cached version available.

Why isn't the weather data updating?
Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or restarting the app.



7. Contact Us for Support


If you need further assistance or have any questions, feel free to Contact Us through the app. You can reach out via email or use the live chat feature for immediate support.
